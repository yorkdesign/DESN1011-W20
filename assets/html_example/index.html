Using Chrome's inspect element

Let's start by inspecting the code on this page. Right click anywhere on this page and select "Inspect" from the list of options that appears. A dock with appear on the right of the page by default. I recommend clicking the three option dots next to the "x" in the top right of that panel. Where it says "Dock side," select the first option. This will pop the dock out as a separate window, giving your browser window a little more space. It's a personal preference. So up to you.

Back to the movie:

The movie Alien came out in 1979, starring a young Sigourney Weaver. The Alien and its accompanying artifacts were designed by the Swiss artist H. R. Giger.

Alien is more a classic horror movie than compared to its follow-up popular Aliens.This movie was directed by relatively unknown at the time, Ridley Scott. It was regarded first as a classic "B movie" horror. However, as it gained in popularity, James Cameron took on the helm to direct Aliens, the more action focused sequel.
